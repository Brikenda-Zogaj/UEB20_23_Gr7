Villas

Villas are a popular choice in the real estate market, especially for those seeking luxury, comfort, and privacy. A villa typically refers to a standalone, upscale residential structure, often characterized by spacious layouts, elegant designs, and high-end amenities

House

Houses are a fundamental aspect of the real estate market and cater to a wide range of preferences and needs. A house typically refers to a single-family dwelling, providing living space separate from other residences.

Penthouse

Penthouses represent a prestigious segment in the real estate market, known for their exclusivity and luxury. A penthouse is typically a high-end apartment or unit situated on the top floor of a building, offering panoramic views and premium features.
